India

India, located in South Asia, is the seventh-largest country and the second-most populous, with over 1.4 billion people. Known for its rich history and cultural diversity, India is home to multiple religions, languages, and ethnic groups. Major religions include Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, and Sikhism, with Hindi and English as the most widely spoken languages. The country boasts a diverse landscape, from the Himalayas in the north to coastal regions along the east and west. India has a mixed economy, with agriculture, manufacturing, and a rapidly growing service sector, particularly in IT. Tourism flourishes, with iconic landmarks like the Taj Mahal and cultural experiences rooted in festivals such as Diwali and Holi. India's vibrant traditions, culinary diversity, and warm hospitality make it a captivating destination.
