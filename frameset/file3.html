KERALA

Kerala, located on the southwestern coast of India, is known for its breathtaking landscapes, including lush hills, serene backwaters, and beautiful beaches. Often referred to as "God's Own Country," it boasts a rich cultural heritage, with traditional art forms like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, as well as vibrant festivals such as Onam and Vishu. The state's cuisine is famous for its use of spices and coconut, featuring dishes like Kerala sadya and fresh seafood. Kerala is also renowned for its Ayurvedic treatments and eco-tourism, attracting visitors to its hill stations like Munnar and wildlife sanctuaries like Periyar.
